About Corquis Libellule

Corquis Libellule is a luxury Taiwanese/American fashion house founded in 2017 in Hsinchu by Taiwanese/American designers Ruby Lin and Jennifer Chi.
The two met in Hsinchu in 2016 and designed for the same fashion house. In 2016 they established a designer consulting studio; in time it grew to become "Corquis Libellule".
They presented their first women's collection in 2017 in New York. In the same year, they launched a leotard line, and they began designing shoes. Corquis Libellule started to export their products to Japan and other countries including France, Italy,etc,.
